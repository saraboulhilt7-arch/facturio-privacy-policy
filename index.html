Facturio – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 November 2025

1. Introduction

Facturio (“the App”) helps users generate invoices, manage clients, and track rides. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

a) Account Information (Supabase Auth)

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account creation date

b) Business Information (Supabase Database)

  • Company name
  • Client details (name, phone, invoices)
  • Invoice and ride details

c) Uploaded Files (Supabase Storage)

  • Company logos
  • PDF invoices

d) Payments

  • Google Play Billing (Android)
  • Apple In-App Purchase (iOS)
  • RevenueCat SDK (subscription management)

We do not store full credit card numbers or payment credentials.

e) Transactional Emails (Resend)

  • Welcome emails
  • Password reset emails
  • Account notifications

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for:

  • Account creation and management
  • Generating and storing invoices
  • Managing subscriptions
  • Sending account-related emails
  • Securing the app and preventing abuse

We do not sell or share data with advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on Supabase (EU data centers) with:

  • Encrypted passwords
  • Secure database communication (TLS)
  • Restricted access

5. Sharing of Data

We only share limited data with these services to operate the app:

Service Purpose
Supabase Auth Login and account management
Supabase Database Storing invoices, clients, rides
Supabase Storage Uploading logos and PDFs
RevenueCat Subscription syncing
Google Play Billing Android purchases
Apple IAP iOS purchases
Resend Sending emails

No data is shared with advertisers.

6. User Rights

Users can access, delete, or update their data through the app interface.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
